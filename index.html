About

Hi, I'm Volkanogeday. I'm a 14 year old guy who loves coding, music, piano and designing. My interest to tech started when I was 4. All those flawless graphics on screens fascinated me and made me beg to my parents for a tablet. That's how my tech journey started. I was gifted with a Android 4.0 tablet.

 

As expected, I was just playing games on my tablet for a long time. One day in 2015 while I was playing games again, an ad popped up from nowhere on my screen. It was a fancy-looking launcher ad. I liked how it looked and downloaded it. That day i spent my game time tinkering the launcher. After some time, I found out there were more of them out there. I made many unique combinations with all those icon packs and launchers. But over the time, 1 or 2 years later, I felt launchers were not enough. I searched how can I make my setups look better and every article was pointing out to one thing, rooting.

 

As I was a curious kid, I searched about rooting and decided to root my tablet to do whatever I want. I tried to use those one click root apps as I didn't had a computer back then. None of them worked as they were either incompatible or just a chinese spyware. After many tries, I gave up. Then in 2018, I got my first laptop Lenovo Ideapad 320 (I call it Ideashit) and new my tablet Galaxy Tab A6. Theese were big upgrades and new possibilities for me. I learned how to install and use linux, how to fix windows when its broken, hackintoshing, debugging errors, what to delete and what not to delete. On android side, I rooted my tablet for the first time in 2020, but I soft bricked it in less then a hour. Luckily i had enough information to fix it. Since then, rooting and installing custom roms is my hobby.

 

At the last quarter of 2021 I ceased living and started to work for highschool exam. Though it was boring for most of the time, night studies and my friend groups made that days fun. After a long period of studying, the school year concluded with the chance of entering one of the best highschools in my city, a new, better laptop and a nice summer holiday. Now my goals are to read more books, learning C#, getting ready for university exam to be accepted into Boğaziçi University, computer engineering section, which accepts the first 300.
