My Story

My interest to tech started when I was 4. All those flawless graphics fascinated me and made me beg to my parents for a tablet. That's how my tech journey started. I was gifted with a Android 4.0 tablet.

 

As expected, I was just playing games on my tablet for a long time since I got my tablet. One day in 2016, a launcher ad popped up on my screen while I was playing games. I liked how it looked and downloaded it. That day I spent my game time dealing with the launcher. After some time, I found out there were more of theese customization apps. I tried many combinations with all those icon packs and launchers. But over the time, I felt launchers were not enough. I searched how can I make my setups look better and most of the guides needed root to work.

 

As I was a curious kid, I searched about rooting and decided to root my tablet to do whatever I want on it. I tried to use those one click root apps as I didn't had a computer back then. None of them worked as they were either incompatible or just a chinese spyware. After many tries, I gave up. Then in 2018, I got my first laptop and a newer tablet. Theese were big upgrades for me compared to my old tablet. I learned how to install and use linux, how to fix windows when its broken, hackintoshing, debugging errors, what to delete and what not to delete. On android side, I rooted my tablet for the first time in 2020 and I soft bricked it in less then a hour. Luckily i was able to fix it. Since then, rooting and installing custom roms is my hobby. I even tried triplebooting linux/android on Galaxy Note 10.1 and i quite succeeded.

 

In 2021, I asked myself why I don't use my tech information to create valueable things and i started creating small os concepts at first. Then some months later, I decided I should start coding, but I failed. Then in september, school started and gave up trying. This year in June, I wanted to give myself one more chance to code and I think I'm really going good this time.
